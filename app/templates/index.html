Developer Student Club

University of Cape Town

As the first Developer Student Club in South Africa, DSC UCT prides itself on preparing student developers for the real world, in the best way possible. We teach people how to code, and turn coders into developers by exposing them to the tools and technologies used in industry. Our developers then become drivers of change, as they use what they have learnt to build tech solutions to local and global issues.
